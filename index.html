About

The Story So Far

I'm a PhD researcher at Universitat Autonoma de Barcelona studying how university teachers develop digital competence — with a decade of hands-on teaching experience in Pakistan.

I'm the founder of PakEdX, an AI-powered platform helping Pakistani teachers teach better with technology. I also built CAITA and QualCoder Pro — open research tools that make qualitative analysis transparent and reproducible.

Previously: classroom teacher and assistant education officer in Punjab. I believe the best EdTech is built by people who've been in the classroom.
